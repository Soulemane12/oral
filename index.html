Proper Brushing Technique

1

Hold your brush at a 45-degree angle to your gums

2

Use gentle circular motions

3

Brush all surfaces of each tooth

4

Don't forget your tongue!

Flossing Guide

1

Break off about 18 inches of floss

2

Wind most of the floss around your middle fingers

3

Gently guide the floss between teeth using a rubbing motion

4

Curve the floss around each tooth in a C shape

Nutrition for Healthy Teeth

1

Eat calcium-rich foods (dairy, leafy greens)

2

Limit sugary and acidic foods

3

Drink plenty of water

4

Choose fiber-rich fruits and vegetables
